Privacy Policy

Abhayattcc Dictionary Reader respects your privacy. This policy outlines how we handle your data:

  • Data Collection: We collect minimal data, such as clipboard content for dictionary lookups and files you upload for OCR or reading.
  • Data Usage: Data is used solely to provide app functionality, such as dictionary searches, OCR, and text-to-speech.
  • Data Storage: Dictionary databases are stored locally on your device. No personal data is sent to our servers.
  • Third Parties: We use Google ML Kit for OCR, Google AdMob for ads, and Google Play Billing for in-app purchases. These services may collect data as per their privacy policies.
  • Permissions: The app requires storage access for database storage, system alert window for clipboard popups, and notifications for background monitoring.
  • Contact: For questions, contact us at ttccabhaya@gmail.com
